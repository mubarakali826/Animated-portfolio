Insights

I talked with stakeholders to understand their biggest pain points and figure out what was actually going wrong. At the same time, I interviewed people from record label teams to get the inside scoop on how their business worked, what their day-to-day looked like, and where they were struggling. All these conversations helped me create role maps and workflow diagrams that would actually solve real problems and make people's jobs easier.

"Bringing teams together in real time wasn't just about speed—it was about enabling smarter decisions and better collaboration." — Scott Cohen, CIO

Low-Fidelity Design

Through multiple iterations, usability testing, and continuous feedback, I refined the interface to ensure clarity, efficiency, and ease of use. By understanding the tech stack's capabilities and limitations, I focused on achievable solutions that maximized resources and delivered real value.

"Edward's leadership guides the team to excel in complex projects, fostering a collaborative and supportive work environment." — Christian Aendekerk, Software Engineer

Story Management Interface

Through multiple iterations, user research, and stakeholder feedback, I designed a streamlined story management system that balances content discovery with administrative control. By understanding the platform's social dynamics and user permissions, I focused on scalable solutions that enhanced content visibility while maintaining appropriate access controls and user safety

Story Creation Interface

Through iterative prototyping, content creator interviews, and usability testing, I developed an intuitive story creation workflow that reduces cognitive load while maximizing creative potential. By understanding the technical constraints of media handling and tagging systems, I prioritized user-friendly features that simplified content production and delivered measurable engagement improvements.

High-Fidelity Design

Based on user feedback, I created a dynamic feed that showcases artist stories alongside music content and social updates. By organizing information clearly and making interactions simple, I delivered an experience that helps fans stay connected and boosts overall engagement.

Story Creation

After user testing and feedback, I designed a simple story creation form that makes it easy for teams to upload content and share updates. By focusing on clear file handling and smart tagging options, I built an interface that speeds up content creation and helps teams stay organized.

Results

Storybox's story feature became one of the most impactful tools for solving Warner's communication challenges. With 488 active users (24% adoption), it's now a key part of how marketing teams collaborate.

The March 2023 numbers show real impact: users save 1-3 minutes per task, adding up to about 1,952 hours saved monthly across the company. With 571 active projects running, teams save 27-60 minutes daily by cutting out scattered communications.

While stories was just one feature in the broader Storybox platform, it proved essential for breaking down the communication barriers that were slowing teams down. The steady growth in usage and time savings shows this feature hit the mark in modernizing how Warner's global marketing teams work together.
